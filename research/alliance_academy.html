Alliance Academy

Graphic Design, Photography, Java

As a member of the faculty at Alliance Academy in Quito, Ecuador, I taught programming classes as well as courses in graphic design and video production. I also served as the yearbook advisor and unofficial school photographer.

In my AP Computer Science course, I taught the basics of object-oriented programming in Java. In my other classes, I taught students how to creating engaging designs using Photoshop, Lightroom, and InDesign.

I served as yearbook advisor from 2012 to 2014 and drastically improved the quality of the yearbooks, while delivering 600 full-color yearbooks to students ahead of schedule and on budget. I personally designed the covers and built page templates for the students to utilize.
