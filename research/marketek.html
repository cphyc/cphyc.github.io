Marketek

Web Design, jQuery, WordPress

Marketek's original website was built with Flash more than 10 years ago. The team brought me in to modernize and redesign the site, making it mobile-friendly and more visually interesting.

Three design mockups were provided, one was chosen, and the site is currently being built in WordPress using the latest HTML5/CSS3 web standards.

The site is currently in beta.
